  O1 --> O2 --> O3
  O2 --> O4

Use when: You’re pitching benefits, not architecture.

Tips to keep it executive:

5–7 nodes max per chart. Use verbs on the arrows.

No acronyms unless essential (AAM/DCL only if you must).

Outcomes over components: add what it enables on each box (e.g., “Normalize, Drift-Repair”).

Keep a single slide per message: Layers, Swimlanes, or Outcomes—don’t mix.
